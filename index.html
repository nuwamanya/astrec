Niche Afri-skills Training & Research Consults Limited (Astrec)

Motto

Hands-on Skills for Everyone

Vision

To be a centre/organ/entity for up to-date skills training and research for innovation for world of Work

Mission

To provide hand-on skills training that conforms to competence based education and training and research for innovation and development in technical vocational and other related fields

Objectives

To support development training documents and materials for all forms of TVET in conformity with competence based principles
Equip trainers/instructors with competences to plan, deliver training for TVET formal, non –formal and other forms of learning that conform to Competence Based Education Training (CBET).
Provide training on competence based assessment(CBA)

To provide training on developing industry lead occupational competence assessment tools and instruments
To conduct training needs assessment, feasibility studies, surveys, capacity assessment, project evaluation, interviews for TVET and other related fields
Spearhead and initiate action research as an approach for solving problems encountered in teaching and work practices.
To mentor, supervise, and support researchers.

To support academic writing and publication
To encourage female participation in TVET disciplines and general education
To offer inspirational and motivational speeches on educational matters both academic and non-academic issues
To conduct career guidance and counselling on TVET issues and general academia programmes
